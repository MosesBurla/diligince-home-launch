# Diligince HUB

## Overview
The Diligince HUB is a marketplace for discovering, evaluating, and connecting with vendors and professionals. Industry users can search for service providers, view profiles, check verification status, and invite vendors to quote on requirements.

> **Note:** Access to Diligince HUB requires the DILIGENCE_HUB subscription feature.

## Pages & Navigation

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Find Vendors | `/dashboard/Diligince-hub/vendors` | Browse and search for vendor companies |
| Find Professionals | `/dashboard/Diligince-hub/professionals` | Browse and search for individual professionals |

## Key Features
- **Vendor Search** — Search vendors by name, category, location, specialization
- **Professional Search** — Find individual experts and consultants
- **Profile Browsing** — View detailed vendor/professional profiles
- **Verification Badges** — See verification status (Basic, Verified, Premium Partner)
- **Ratings & Reviews** — View ratings and reviews from other Industry users
- **Invite to Quote** — Directly invite vendors to submit quotations for your requirements
- **Direct Connect** — Start a conversation with any vendor or professional
- **Save/Bookmark** — Save vendors to your favorites for future reference

## How To: Search for Vendors
### Steps
1. Navigate to [Find Vendors](/dashboard/Diligince-hub/vendors)
2. Use the search bar to search by name or keyword
3. Apply filters: category, location, verification level, rating, experience
4. Browse vendor profiles in the results
5. Click on a vendor to view their full profile

## How To: Invite a Vendor to Quote
### Steps
1. Go to [Find Vendors](/dashboard/Diligince-hub/vendors)
2. Find and open the vendor's profile
3. Click "Invite to Quote"
4. Select the requirement you want them to quote on
5. The vendor receives a notification and can submit a quotation

## Common Questions

**Q:** How do I find vendors on Diligince.ai?
**A:** Use the [Diligince HUB - Vendors](/dashboard/Diligince-hub/vendors) to search and discover vendors.

**Q:** How do I find individual professionals?
**A:** Go to [Diligince HUB - Professionals](/dashboard/Diligince-hub/professionals) to browse experts.

**Q:** Can I invite a vendor to quote on my requirement?
**A:** Yes, open the vendor's profile in the [HUB](/dashboard/Diligince-hub/vendors) and click "Invite to Quote."

**Q:** What do verification badges mean?
**A:** Vendors can be Basic (email verified), Verified (documents checked), or Premium Partner (fully vetted).

**Q:** Do I need a subscription for the HUB?
**A:** Yes, Diligince HUB access requires the DILIGENCE_HUB subscription feature. Check your [Subscription](/dashboard/subscription/plans).
