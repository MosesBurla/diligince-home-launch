# Industry Subscription Module

## Overview
The Subscription module lets Industry users view their current plan, explore available features, upgrade or downgrade plans, and view payment transaction history.

## Pages & Navigation

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| My Plan | `/dashboard/subscription/plans` | Current plan and available plans |
| Transactions | `/dashboard/subscription/transactions` | Payment history |
| Transaction Detail | `/dashboard/subscription/transactions/:id` | Details of a specific transaction |

## Key Features
- **Plan Overview** — View current subscription plan and included features
- **Plan Comparison** — Compare available plans side by side
- **Upgrade/Downgrade** — Change subscription plan
- **Payment History** — View all past transactions
- **Feature Access** — Some features (like Diligince HUB) require specific plan features

## How To: View Your Current Plan
### Steps
1. Navigate to [My Plan](/dashboard/subscription/plans)
2. View your current plan details and included features
3. Compare with other available plans

## How To: View Payment History
### Steps
1. Go to [Transactions](/dashboard/subscription/transactions)
2. Browse your payment history
3. Click on any transaction to view its [details](/dashboard/subscription/transactions/:id)

## Common Questions

**Q:** Where can I see my subscription plan?
**A:** Go to [My Plan](/dashboard/subscription/plans) to view your current plan.

**Q:** How do I upgrade my plan?
**A:** Visit [My Plan](/dashboard/subscription/plans), compare plans, and click "Upgrade."

**Q:** Where is my payment history?
**A:** Go to [Transactions](/dashboard/subscription/transactions) for all payment records.

**Q:** Why can't I access Diligince HUB?
**A:** Diligince HUB requires the DILIGENCE_HUB feature. Check your plan at [My Plan](/dashboard/subscription/plans) and upgrade if needed.
