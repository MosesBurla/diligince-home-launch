# Service Vendor Services Module

## Overview
The Services module allows Service Vendors to manage their service catalog — the list of services, skills, and expertise they offer to Industry buyers. A well-maintained service catalog improves visibility in the Diligince HUB and helps match with relevant RFQs.

## Pages & Navigation

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Service Catalog | `/dashboard/service-vendor-services` | Manage your services and expertise |

## Key Features
- **Service Listing** — Add and manage the services you offer
- **Skills & Expertise** — Define your areas of expertise
- **Categories** — Organize services by category
- **Visibility** — Services appear on your vendor profile in the Diligince HUB

## How To: Add a Service
### Steps
1. Navigate to [Service Catalog](/dashboard/service-vendor-services)
2. Click "Add Service"
3. Enter service name, description, and category
4. Add relevant skills and tags
5. Save the service

## How To: Update Your Service Catalog
### Steps
1. Go to [Service Catalog](/dashboard/service-vendor-services)
2. Click on any service to edit its details
3. Update description, category, or tags
4. Save changes

## Common Questions

**Q:** Where do I manage my services?
**A:** Go to [Service Catalog](/dashboard/service-vendor-services).

**Q:** How do buyers find my services?
**A:** Your services appear on your profile in the [Diligince HUB](/dashboard/Diligince-hub/vendors) for Industry buyers to discover.

**Q:** Can I add multiple services?
**A:** Yes, add as many services as you offer at [Service Catalog](/dashboard/service-vendor-services).
