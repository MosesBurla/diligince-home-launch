# Service Vendor - Platform Overview

## Overview
Service Vendors are companies that provide services, products, or expertise to Industry buyers on Diligince.ai. The platform enables vendors to browse RFQs, submit quotations, manage projects, communicate with clients, and grow their business.

## Vendor Workflow

1. **Browse RFQs** → Find relevant requirements ([Browse RFQs](/dashboard/service-vendor-rfqs))
2. **Submit Quotation** → Respond with pricing and terms ([Submit Quotation](/dashboard/rfqs/:rfqId/submit-quotation))
3. **Track Quotations** → Monitor quotation status ([All Quotations](/dashboard/vendor/quotations))
4. **Execute Projects** → Deliver on accepted POs ([Active Projects](/dashboard/service-vendor-projects/active))
5. **Complete & Get Paid** → Finish deliverables and receive payment

## Common Questions

**Q:** Where do I start as a new vendor?
**A:** Set up your profile at [Vendor Profile](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile), add services at [Service Catalog](/dashboard/service-vendor-services), then browse [RFQs](/dashboard/service-vendor-rfqs).

**Q:** How do I find work?
**A:** Browse available RFQs at [Browse RFQs](/dashboard/service-vendor-rfqs) and submit quotations for relevant ones.

**Q:** Where do I manage my team?
**A:** Go to [Team Members](/dashboard/team/members) and [Role Management](/dashboard/team/roles).
