# Service Vendor Settings Module

## Overview
The Settings module lets Service Vendors configure their company profile, vendor profile (visible to buyers), certifications, portfolio, payment settings, and personal account settings.

## Pages & Navigation

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Company Profile | `/dashboard/vendor-settings` | Basic company information |
| Service Vendor Profile | `/dashboard/service-vendor-profile` | Public vendor profile visible to buyers |
| Certifications | `/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/certifications` | Manage certifications and credentials |
| Projects & Portfolio | `/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/portfolio` | Showcase completed projects |
| Payment Settings | `/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/payment` | Configure payment and banking details |
| Account Settings | `/settings/account-settings` | Personal account, password, security |

## Key Features
- **Company Profile** — Update company name, logo, description, and contact info
- **Public Vendor Profile** — The profile Industry buyers see in the Diligince HUB
- **Certifications** — Upload and manage industry certifications (ISO, safety, quality)
- **Portfolio** — Showcase past projects with images, technologies, and outcomes
- **Payment Settings** — Configure bank details and payment preferences
- **Account Security** — Change password, two-factor authentication

## How To: Update Your Vendor Profile
### Steps
1. Navigate to [Service Vendor Profile](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile)
2. Update your company description, logo, and tagline
3. Add your areas of expertise and key highlights
4. Save changes — this profile is visible to buyers

## How To: Add Certifications
### Steps
1. Go to [Certifications](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/certifications)
2. Click **"Add Certification"**
3. Enter certification name, issuing body, and certification number
4. Set issue date and expiry date
5. Select a category (e.g., Quality Management, Safety, Environmental)
6. Upload the certificate document (PDF, JPG, or PNG — max 10 MB)
7. Click **Save** — certifications appear on your public profile

### Certification Statuses
- **Active** — Current and valid
- **Pending Renewal** — Expiring within 30 days
- **Expired** — Past expiry date, needs renewal

### Managing Certifications
- **Edit**: Click on a certification to update its details
- **Delete**: Remove certifications you no longer hold
- **Replace Document**: Upload a new document to replace the existing one
- **Sort & Filter**: Use status filters and search to find specific certifications

## How To: Build Your Portfolio
### Steps
1. Navigate to [Projects & Portfolio](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/portfolio)
2. Click **"Add Project"**
3. Enter project details:
   - **Project Name** — e.g., "Factory Automation System"
   - **Client Name** (optional) — e.g., "Acme Manufacturing"
   - **Description** — Detailed project overview (10–2000 characters)
   - **Start & End Dates** — Project timeline
   - **Status** — Completed, In Progress, or Planned
   - **Technologies** — Tags for technologies used (e.g., PLC, SCADA, IoT)
   - **Outcomes** — Key results and achievements
   - **Project Value** (optional) — Contract value and currency
4. Upload a project image (JPG, PNG, or WEBP — max 5 MB)
5. Click **Save** — portfolio items appear on your public profile

### Portfolio Views
- **Grid View** — Visual card layout with project images
- **List View** — Compact table layout for quick scanning
- **Filter by Status** — Show completed, in-progress, or planned projects
- **Search** — Find projects by name, client, or technology

## How To: Configure Payment Settings
### Steps
1. Go to [Payment Settings](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/payment)
2. Enter your bank account or payment details
3. Configure payment preferences
4. Save changes

## Common Questions

**Q:** How do I update my company profile?
**A:** Go to [Company Profile](/dashboard/vendor-settings) for basic info or [Vendor Profile](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile) for your public profile.

**Q:** Where do I add certifications?
**A:** Go to [Certifications](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/certifications). You can add, edit, delete, and upload certificate documents.

**Q:** How do I showcase my past work?
**A:** Add projects to your [Portfolio](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/portfolio). Include descriptions, technologies, outcomes, and images.

**Q:** Where do I set up payment details?
**A:** Go to [Payment Settings](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/payment).

**Q:** Where are my personal account settings?
**A:** Go to [Account Settings](/settings/account-settings) for password, security, and personal profile.

**Q:** Do certifications and portfolio appear on my public profile?
**A:** Yes. Active certifications and completed projects are visible to Industry buyers on the Diligince HUB.

**Q:** What file formats are supported for certificate uploads?
**A:** PDF, JPG, and PNG files up to 10 MB.

**Q:** What image formats are supported for portfolio projects?
**A:** JPG, PNG, and WEBP images up to 5 MB.

**Q:** Will I be notified when a certification is about to expire?
**A:** Yes. Certifications are marked as "Pending Renewal" 30 days before expiry.
