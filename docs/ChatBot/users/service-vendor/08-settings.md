# Service Vendor Settings Module

## Overview
The Settings module lets Service Vendors configure their company profile, vendor profile (visible to buyers), certifications, portfolio, payment settings, and personal account settings.

## Pages & Navigation

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Company Profile | `/dashboard/vendor-settings` | Basic company information |
| Service Vendor Profile | `/dashboard/service-vendor-profile` | Public vendor profile visible to buyers |
| Certifications | `/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/certifications` | Manage certifications and credentials |
| Projects & Portfolio | `/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/portfolio` | Showcase completed projects |
| Payment Settings | `/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/payment` | Configure payment and banking details |
| Account Settings | `/settings/account-settings` | Personal account, password, security |

## Key Features
- **Company Profile** — Update company name, logo, description, and contact info
- **Public Vendor Profile** — The profile Industry buyers see in the Diligince HUB
- **Certifications** — Upload and manage industry certifications
- **Portfolio** — Showcase past projects and completed work
- **Payment Settings** — Configure bank details and payment preferences
- **Account Security** — Change password, two-factor authentication

## How To: Update Your Vendor Profile
### Steps
1. Navigate to [Service Vendor Profile](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile)
2. Update your company description, logo, and tagline
3. Add your areas of expertise and key highlights
4. Save changes — this profile is visible to buyers

## How To: Add Certifications
### Steps
1. Go to [Certifications](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/certifications)
2. Click "Add Certification"
3. Enter certification name, issuing body, and validity dates
4. Upload certificate document
5. Save — certifications appear on your public profile

## How To: Build Your Portfolio
### Steps
1. Navigate to [Portfolio](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/portfolio)
2. Click "Add Project"
3. Enter project name, description, client (optional), and outcomes
4. Upload images or documents
5. Save — portfolio items appear on your public profile

## How To: Configure Payment Settings
### Steps
1. Go to [Payment Settings](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/payment)
2. Enter your bank account or payment details
3. Configure payment preferences
4. Save changes

## Common Questions

**Q:** How do I update my company profile?
**A:** Go to [Company Profile](/dashboard/vendor-settings) for basic info or [Vendor Profile](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile) for your public profile.

**Q:** Where do I add certifications?
**A:** Go to [Certifications](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/certifications).

**Q:** How do I showcase my past work?
**A:** Add projects to your [Portfolio](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/portfolio).

**Q:** Where do I set up payment details?
**A:** Go to [Payment Settings](/dashboard/service-vendor-profile/payment).

**Q:** Where are my personal account settings?
**A:** Go to [Account Settings](/settings/account-settings) for password, security, and personal profile.
