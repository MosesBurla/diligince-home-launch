# Service Vendor Dashboard

## Overview
The Service Vendor Dashboard provides an at-a-glance overview of your business on Diligince.ai, including stats, team availability, RFQ management, active projects, and recent messages.

## Pages & Navigation

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Vendor Dashboard | `/dashboard/service-vendor` | Main dashboard with business overview |

## Key Features
- **Business Stats** — Total quotations, active projects, revenue, win rate
- **Team Availability** — Overview of team members and their current assignments
- **RFQ Management** — Recent RFQs and pending quotations
- **Active Projects** — Projects currently in progress
- **Message Center** — Recent messages from clients

## Page Details

### Vendor Dashboard
**Route:** `/dashboard/service-vendor`
**Description:** Central hub with business metrics, team status, and quick access to key actions.
**Available Actions:**
- View business statistics
- Check team availability
- Browse latest RFQs
- Access active projects
- Read recent messages
- Quick navigation to all modules

## How To: Navigate the Dashboard
### Steps
1. Go to [Dashboard](/dashboard/service-vendor)
2. Review your business stats at the top
3. Check team availability in the team section
4. See latest RFQs and pending quotations
5. Monitor active projects progress
6. Read and respond to recent messages

## Common Questions

**Q:** Where is my vendor dashboard?
**A:** Go to [Dashboard](/dashboard/service-vendor) for your complete business overview.

**Q:** How do I see my team's availability?
**A:** The Team Availability section on the [Dashboard](/dashboard/service-vendor) shows current assignments.

**Q:** Where can I see new RFQs?
**A:** The dashboard shows recent RFQs, or go to [Browse RFQs](/dashboard/service-vendor-rfqs) for the full list.
