# Service Vendor Subscription Module

## Overview
The Subscription module lets Service Vendors view their current plan, explore available features, upgrade plans, and view payment transaction history.

## Pages & Navigation

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| My Plan | `/dashboard/subscription/plans` | Current plan and available plans |
| Transactions | `/dashboard/subscription/transactions` | Payment history |

## Key Features
- **Plan Overview** — View your current subscription and included features
- **Plan Comparison** — Compare available plans
- **Upgrade** — Upgrade to access more features
- **Payment History** — View past transactions and invoices

## How To: View Your Plan
### Steps
1. Navigate to [My Plan](/dashboard/subscription/plans)
2. Review your current plan and features
3. Compare with other available plans

## How To: View Payment History
### Steps
1. Go to [Transactions](/dashboard/subscription/transactions)
2. Browse your payment history and invoices

## Common Questions

**Q:** Where can I see my subscription?
**A:** Go to [My Plan](/dashboard/subscription/plans).

**Q:** How do I upgrade my plan?
**A:** Visit [My Plan](/dashboard/subscription/plans) and click "Upgrade."

**Q:** Where is my payment history?
**A:** Go to [Transactions](/dashboard/subscription/transactions).
